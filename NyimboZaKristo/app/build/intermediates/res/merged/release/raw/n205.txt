No. 205: Msifu Mungu Ee Watoto


Msifu Mungu, Ee watoto wote,
Yu pendo, Yu pendo;
Msifu, Msifu, Ee watoto wote
Mungu ni upendo.

Tunampenda, Ee watoto wote,
Yu pendo, Yu pendo,
Tunampenda, Ee watoto wote,
Mungu ni upendo.

Tumikeni, Ee watoto wote,
Yu pendo, Yu pendo;
Tumikeni, Ee watoto wote,
Mungu ni upendo.
